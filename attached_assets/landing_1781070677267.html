How it works

Three steps. Bots never see it coming.

Instead of blocking bots outright (which tips them off), we use a deceptive honeypot mechanism that lets bots identify themselves.

Step 01
🔍

Score every request

Each booking request is scored 0–100 for bot probability based on typing speed, mouse movement, IP patterns, and request velocity.

Step 02
🍯

Route bots to honeypot

High-probability bots are shown fake ticket slots instead of real ones. They try to confirm the fake booking — we let them.

Step 03
✅

Protect genuine users

Confirmed bots get blacklisted across sessions. Real passengers are fast-tracked. 94% genuine success rate vs 23% without this system.
