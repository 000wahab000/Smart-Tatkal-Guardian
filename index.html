Bot Probability Scoring

Each booking request gets a 0–100 bot probability score based on typing speed, mouse movement, IP patterns, and request velocity.

🍯

Honeypot Mechanism

Suspected bots are shown fake ticket slots. They confirm fake bookings — we log them. After 3 confirmations, permanently blacklisted.

Real-time Detection

1000 simultaneous booking requests processed in real-time. 800 bots identified and routed. 200 genuine users get priority access.
